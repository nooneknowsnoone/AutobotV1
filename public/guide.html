Step-By-Step Guide

1. Install the Kiwi Browser from the Google Play Store and proceed to launch the application.

2. Navigate to the search and enter: https://github.com/c3cbot/c3c-fbstate/archive/refs/tags/1.5.zip

3. Click the download option once prompted.

4. Tap the three dots in the top-right, then select "Extensions".

5. Enable Developer Mode and click the "+" to add the extension.

6. Locate and select the downloaded zip file to install.

7. Activate the C3C fbstate utility from the bottom-right corner.

8. Go to Facebook.com and log into your account.

9. Open the three-dot menu, scroll down, and select the C3C fbstate utility.

10. On the redirected page, click "Copy Clipboard" to copy your cookies.

11. Paste the cookies at https://auto-bot-soyeon.onrender.com and review the Terms and Privacy.

12. Check the box and submit after reading the policy.

13. Type "ai" to confirm bot status. If it replies, setup is complete.


Note:

• If login fails, unlock the account and retry.

• Avoid logging in on other devices while bot is active.

• Don’t change the password or log in while bot is active.

• We’re not liable for account locks or platform actions.

• To register another bot, logout (not die appstate), then repeat.
